About PRISM

Class it up, bring a friend and enjoy a night filled with pieces designed to inform and inspire. This May 11th, SuperCouncil Area D is hosting an art social event, consisting of live performances and student made art made to bring awareness to mental health and the mental illnesses that affect 10-20% of all Canadian youth. This networking event will give you the opportunity to meet and mingle with students from across Toronto.

RSVP on Facebook

Where and When

PRISM will take place at Wave Gallery in Earl Haig Secondary School on Wednesday, May 11 from 6 PM to 9 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find tickets?

Almost all schools in the North Eastern part of toronto has designated event ambassadors that keep tickets. These ambassadors are listed (with their school name) on the who we are page. Feel free to contact them either through social media or in the halls.

I can't find my ambassador, is there any other way I can get tickets/contact someone?

Feel free to contact us through our online form on the contact us page.

Can I buy tickets at the door?

Tickets cannot be purchased at the doors of the event, due to there being a limited number of spots and security arrangements.

What is the official dress code?

This is a semi-formal event, meaning no sneakers or jeans.

I have something else to attend to on the day of the event, can I get a refund?

As each ticket is billed out to a specific person, refunds are not available. Please consider all engagements before purchasing your ticket.

I want to perform or display art at the event. How do I do that?

You can do that by following the instructions on the artists page.
