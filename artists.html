Share your work.

Whether you are a painter, a musician, or even a spoken word artist, we will give you a chance to shine. On May 11th, the TDSB SuperCouncil Area D is hosting an art exhibition and networking gala for mental health and awareness. During this event, we want to show off your artistic skills. Fill out the form below to be considered for this great opportunity.

Application in Person

Click here to download the application.

Online Application

Apply below if you want your artwork (relating to mental health) to be on display at PRISM or if you want to perform at PRISM.


*You must be a TDSB student to be considered.

Although creativity is encouraged, please refrain from any overly explicit content.

If you have any questions, you can contact us.

Applications close on Sunday, May 8th at 11:59PM (UPDATED).


All fields required unless otherwise indicated.


If you are submitting an artpiece, please email us (adip.prism@gmail.com) an image of your artpiece with your name and a comment stating that you are submitting your art.

OR

RECOMMENDED: Upload your artwork to Google Drive or Dropbox and put its link here.

If you are submitting a piece of art (i.e. a drawing, a photograph, a video, etc...), you are promising that you own all rights to all material in your art piece. You agree to indemnify the PRISM event, including for legal costs, against any challenges to the ownership, use of, or rights to material in your video. Upon the approval of your submission of your art piece to PRISM, the TDSB SuperCouncil retains the right to use the media for advertisement and exhibition for the current event.
Upon approval of your art piece, our team will contact you to find a suitable location to pick up your art. As well, our team will notify ambassadors about your free ticket.
